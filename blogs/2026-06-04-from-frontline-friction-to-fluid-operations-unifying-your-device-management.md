The frontline, once disconnected from the digital core of organizations, now stands as the epicenter of value creation as businesses equip frontline workers with mobile technology to improve customer service and logistics. Tom Schmidt argues that managing distributed device fleets effectively is the critical bottleneck preventing organizations from realizing the full value of this investment. The post outlines strategies for unifying device management to eliminate frontline friction and enable fluid operations.
