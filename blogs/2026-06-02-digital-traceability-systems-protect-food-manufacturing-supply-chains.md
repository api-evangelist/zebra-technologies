Product recalls represent a massive threat to food and beverage manufacturers, with the average direct cost of a single incident estimated at $10 million and indirect costs inflicting deeper, longer-lasting damage across the supply chain. Digital traceability systems enable manufacturers to pinpoint affected batches rapidly, limiting recall scope and protecting brand reputation. The post covers how Zebra Technologies' solutions support end-to-end traceability in food manufacturing supply chains.
